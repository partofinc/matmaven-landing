Privacy Policy for MatMaven

At MatMaven, we take your privacy seriously. This privacy policy outlines the types of personal information we may collect from you, how we use that information, and the steps we take to protect your information.

1. Information we may collect

We may collect the following types of personal information from you:

  • Name and contact information (such as email address) when you create an account or contact us for support
  • User-generated content (such as Jiu Jitsu match data) that you input into the app
  • Usage information, such as how often you use the app and how you use it

2. How we use your information

We use your personal information to:

  • Provide and improve our app and services
  • Communicate with you about the app and updates
  • Respond to your support requests
  • Comply with any legal obligations

We will not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your personal information with third-party service providers who assist us in providing the app and services, such as hosting and data analysis. These service providers are bound by strict confidentiality obligations and may only use your personal information to provide services to us.

3. Data retention

We will retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide the app and services to you. After that, we will securely delete your information or anonymize it for statistical purposes.

4. Security measures

We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. These measures include, but are not limited to, encryption, access controls, and regular security assessments.

5. Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, and delete your personal information at any time. You also have the right to object to or restrict the processing of your personal information. To exercise these rights or to request more information, please contact us at [insert contact information].

6. Changes to this privacy policy

We reserve the right to update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. If we make any material changes, we will notify you by email or through the app. Your continued use of the app after any such changes will constitute your acceptance of the updated policy.
